Who we're looking for

We only need your commitment and patience (needed in coding). No programming knowledge required to enter this club. We are happy to recruit those who have no experience (or have some) in programming and are interested in programming or coding. This is a lively programming community, where learning is not everything. Members enjoy each others' jokes and build connections and friendships. As we welcome you as our new brother/sister, we hope that you not only stay persistent in learning programming with us, but also contribute in collaborative learning
